Questions? Here's some answers!

Will there be food and drinks!?

Yes! Thanks to the unrelenting and incredible support from our sponsors we can provide free pizza and refreshments which will arrive at the meetings at 6.30pm. Get there on time to avoid missing out!

When do we go to the pub!?

As soon as we’ve all finished presenting and eating, we’ll head to the pub.

What do you mean by talk?

A talk doesn’t necessarily have to be a presentation, it could merely be a discussion point or quick tip / lesson learnt.

What can I talk about?

Anything that happens in the lifecycle of Mobile Development. So design, development, testing, requirements. Anything newsworthy about the mobile sector you’d like to discuss.

How long can I talk for?

Anything from 30 seconds to 20 minutes, inc Q and A. This is to keep the talks short and punchy. If you have a larger topic you would like to discuss contact us at mobiledorset@gmail.com

What time should I arrive?

5:30pm onwards. Hot drinks will be available and mingling. Talks and pizza will be at 6.30pm

How often do you hold events

Events are held every second Wednesday of the month and will usually be held in the EBC

Who

Mobile Dorset is brought to you by Alec Holmes, James Newman and Phillip Caudell. The meetups couldn’t happen without Bournemouth University, Base and 3 Sided Cube.
